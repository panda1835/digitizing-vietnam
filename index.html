Digitizing Vietnam

Digitizing Vietnam is a collaborative initiative by Columbia University and Fulbright University Vietnam, funded by the Luce Foundation, aimed at constructing a cutting-edge digital infrastructure for Vietnamese Studies. This endeavor revolves around the development of the Digital Hub, a virtual repository that offers access to digitized premodern manuscripts, modern archival collections in Vietnam, and advanced digital humanities tools.
